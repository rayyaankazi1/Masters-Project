# Literature basis: Alesina & Ardagna (2010), Guajardo et al. (2011),
# Kopits & Symansky (1998) fiscal rules, Barro-Gordon credibility framework.

# REMOVED (zero-hit): ajuste estructural, alivio impositivo,
#   consolidacion presupuestaria, credibilidad fiscal, deficit es inflacion,
#   eliminacion de subsidios, fin de los subsidios, quita de subsidios,
#   reduccion de ministerios, reduccion de subsidios
#   — these concepts appear in the corpus but not via these exact phrasings.
#   NOTE: actualizacion tarifaria was previously zero-hit but re-added below

#
# REMOVED (Milei-specific political branding — not cross-president vocabulary):
#   motosierra, licuadora — fiscal metaphors exclusive to Milei's register;

#   la casta, casta politica, viva la libertad — pure political slogans with
#     no fiscal semantic content (93–100% Milei-specific, 0 fiscal meaning).
